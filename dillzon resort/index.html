Meet the Owner

The Heart Behind Dillzan

Actor & Founder

Founder & Owner

Dillzan Wadia

Actor  ·  Founder & Owner, The Dillzan Resort

🎬 Sarfaroshi 🎬 Four Pillars of Basement 🎬 Bollywood Villa
"My aim is to see people enjoy themselves at my resort and feel like they are in their own home. For my guests, there is no 'No' — we want them to experience warmth, comfort, and freedom just like they would in their own house."

Actor Dillzan Wadia, known for films like Sarfaroshi, Four Pillars of Basement, Bollywood Villa and several television shows, has now successfully transitioned into the world of hospitality with the launch of his premium boutique property — The Dillzan Resort, Alibaug.

Originally from Vadodara, Dillzan moved to Mumbai to pursue acting, supporting himself by running a small fast-food joint at King Circle while studying at Khalsa Junior College. His dedication earned him roles in acclaimed films before his passion for hospitality led him to develop boutique resorts in Goa and Lonavala — and now, Alibaug.

When asked about his natural hosting abilities, Dillzan says: "This quality comes from my parents. At our home, every guest was treated with love and humility, making them feel completely comfortable. I've simply carried forward those values into my profession."

From facing the camera to creating curated guest experiences, Dillzan Wadia's journey reflects his passion for people, perseverance, and a commitment to redefining boutique hospitality in Alibaug.

— Dillzan Wadia
